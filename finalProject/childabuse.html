Child Abuse

What is child abuse ?

Child abuse or child maltreatment is physical, sexual, or psychological maltreatment or neglect of a child or children, especially by a parent or other caregiver.

Fact

Every year more than 3 million reports of child abuse are made in the United States. It is a very bad epidemic that we at Childhelp are dedicated to put an end to. To do this, we need to first increase awareness of the issue itself.
